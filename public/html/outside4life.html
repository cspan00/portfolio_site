Outside4Life

Me and 3 other Galvanize students are working with the CEO of a local-non profit. Users can log in with facebook, upload photos, add descriptions and then select how many hours they spent outdoors. When they reach a certain number of hours in a year, Avid4Adventure will award them prizes. The project is still in active development with a planned deployment in May. My role in the project was primarily backend API and Angular integration
