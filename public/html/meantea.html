MEAN tea

MEAN tea was an exercise assigned to me as part of the Galvanize cirriculum. I was pretty excited at how it turned out, so I included it here. It's a standard shopping cart app made from scratch using Angular. You can add items to a cart, search and sort items by category, and edit and delete items in the cart. Could easily be used as a template for an actual webstore
